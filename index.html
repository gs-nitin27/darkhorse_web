Technology and Services

The world of sport is evolving into a platform where technology has become an inextricable part of the system. The technology not only provides a visual treat on the tv sets to the fans but it also helps the sports people whether they are a coach, parents or the players in achieving their respective targets. So to keep pace with the fast growing technology in the world of sport, we at GetSporty have developed a "Go to platform" for every sport enthusiast. The Technology has shrinked the world into a tiny little room where one can connect with anybody irrespective of his place in the world (unless you live on an isolated island somewhere in the Antarctica). But India still lags behind in terms of technology used by our sports people because either we don't have access or there is no system that can provide a precise information to all the people. So we at GetSporty have developed a platform where all the sports people, wheteher they belong to sport's administration class or just an amateur player, can connect through our unique platform. GetSporty is committed to providing the best service possible to all the people of the world of sport. In order to meet this promise, we are working hard to create a unique platform where the players, coaches or the parents can connect with each other to help create a much needed tranparency in the infamous Indian sport system. When you place your trust in us, here’s what you can expect in return: 1. All the aspiring athletes, coaches and the people related to the world of sport can expect to find the best and the unique information based on their sport 2. We will provide the little extras to all the players, coaches and the parents in the world of sport that will surely help you in making a big difference. 3. We hope to build a loyal relationship between the players, coaches and parents that is based on trust and mutual respect 4. We understand the needs of our users and hope to exceed your expectations. 5. We will always pay heed towards your suggestions and respond to your feedback in a timely manner.
